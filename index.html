Land Parcel Selection

This map illustrated a land parcel was selected for building social security housing for talents in Shenzhen, China. It also showed the criteria that was applied into the filtering process.

Obesity & Latitude

This longform infographic demonstrates how does latitude affect obesity rate in China. Several bivariate choropleth map of obesity rate and tempreture or GDP were made to discover the pattern that people living on the north are more likely to have high obesity rate then those on the south.

Cycling Route

In this Lab I was preparing a promotional map for the newly created European Grand Tour, a men’s and women’s road cycling competition to be held every four years as the sport’s main showcase.I was geographic consultant and the cartographer, and proposed a route for the event and providing a sample map for displaying the route.
